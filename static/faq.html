Frequently Asked Questions


   How do I change or remove a GO link?
Send us your request here.

   Can I share my GO link with non-employees?
Unfortunately no. Only computers attached to our network are able to use the GO service.

   Can GO redirect to a file? (ie. O:\ drive)
In short no.
It's insecure. Browsers were not designed to view files and have restrictions in place to prevent this from being possible. See here for an explanation. It could also be misleading. There isn't a reliable way of knowing whether the person you're sharing the link with has similiar permissions to access the file on the Windows domain.
